NZ Compound Interest Calculator

See how your money could grow over time! Enter your starting amount, monthly contributions, interest rate, and years of saving, then click Calculate.

The amount of money you already have to start with, in NZ dollars. How much you plan to add each month. Use 0 if you won’t add more. The yearly growth of your money. Check your bank account, term deposit, or KiwiSaver fund for this rate. How many years you plan to leave the money invested.

How It Works

The calculator uses standard compound interest, adding monthly contributions and applying the yearly interest rate. It assumes contributions are made at the end of each month.
